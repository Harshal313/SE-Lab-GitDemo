FANDOM

This quiz is all about testing your knowledge about your favorite show. FANDOM gives you a chance to attempt quizzes for the following shows:

A. FRIENDS
B. The Office
C. Harry Potter

This quiz is designed to test the participant’s knowledge about the show attempted for, the characters, the flow and thus is an opportunity to prove that the participant is a true fan of the series.

About Event

ROUND 1:
Multiple Choice Questions.


ROUND 2:
Questions based on identifying the scenes or dialogues, characters or relatable stuff. Similar questions based on videos, audios, signs etc.


Elimination criteria:

Anyone who scores 20/30+ would proceed for Round 2.


Team and fee structure

Team size : Maximum 2 per team.
Registration fee : ₹99 per team per show.


Rules

1. You can attempt FANDOM quiz of one show ONLY ONCE.

2. If a team is caught cheating or playing by unfair means then the team will be disqualified.

3. Participants should report to the venue on time and must attend the quiz in the given slot only.

4. The decision of the respective Quiz Head will be final and will not be subjected to any change.

5. Replacement of any participant is strictly not allowed.


NOTE :

• If number of participants are less than 20 then only 2 winners will be selected.
• If number of participants are less than 5 then only 1 winner will be selected.
• If number of participants are less than or equal to 3 then event will be cancelled.


FAQs

Q. What is the team structure?
A. Participants can take part in a team of maximum 2 players. You can participate individually also but a team of 2 is recommended per show.


Q. Can I participate in other events at MindSpark’22?
A. You are allowed to participate in as many events at MindSpark’22 as long as you are able to manage your own schedule.


Q. What does the Round 2 preparations involve?
A. There are no special preparations required for Round 2. Just ensure that your team has watched the respective show carefully and thoroughly.


Q. Can I participate for different shows?
A. You are allowed to participate for different shows after registering for them. Just ensure that your team able to manage your own schedule.


Q. Can I participate for a single show multiple times?
A. No. You can attempt FANDOM quiz of one show ONLY ONCE.


Q. Should I read books (if available for relevant shows)?
A. Not necessarily. Quiz will have questions based on series only.
